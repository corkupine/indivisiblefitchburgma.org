About Us

Indivisible Fitchburg is part of the national Indivisible movement that began in response to the 2016 election. We are a grassroots group of neighbors committed to building a more inclusive democracy.

Our mission is to resist the agenda that threatens equality, justice, and our democratic institutions. Through local action, we work to create positive change in our community and hold our elected officials accountable.

We believe in the power of organized resistance and that by standing together, we can effect meaningful change in Fitchburg and beyond.
